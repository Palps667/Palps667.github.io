    Bio

    Balon Greyjoy was born between 255 and 261 A.C. on the isle and castle of Pyke, in the Iron Islands. The Ironborn are people that pride themselves on raiding and Balon is a firm believer in the old ways of his people. Balon was a fierce, fearless and bitter man. Balon attempted to make himself king twice in his life. The first time during the [Greyjoy Rebellion]9) and a second time during The War of the Five Kings.Balon would once more declare the Ironborn independent after the death of King Robert and assert his claim to The North in the absence of Robb Stark. The Ironborn would seize several castles and briefly occupy Winterfell. Balon would unfortunately lose his only remaining son Theon as a captive to House Bolton and the Ironborn were slowly pushed out of The North. At the end of 299 A.C. Balon's treacherous younger brother Euron “Crow’s Eye” Greyjoy would return and Balon would die under mysterious circumstances.
